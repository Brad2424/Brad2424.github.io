About Me

Hello world!


My name's Brad and I'm a web-developer from Canterbury, New Zealand. As a graduate of the Treehouse FEWD Techdegree (link to certificate), I am looking to put my programming skills to the test, checkout some of my previous work in the section below!


I'm also graduate from the University of Canterbury with a BCom in Marketing and Management and have 3+ years international experience as an Export Manager with T&G Global. As an Export Manager, I was lucky enough to be able to travel and work with people around the world in places like Thailand, Hong Kong, USA, Australia, Singapore and Vietnam.


Through these international experiences, I developed a real interest in international cultures, where I decided I wanted to live and work in Latin America to broaden my international business skill set (learning Spanish being one of those skills!).


Since living in Latina America for the past 2+ years, I have worked as an online English teacher and as a Business Development Manager at Publicize, a public relations startup. Both were great experiences; however, nothing has captured my attention and spiked my interest quite like programming!
